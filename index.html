Overview

For this project, we will be creating a medical chatbot by the name PMD. PMD is a discord-only pre-diagnosis chatbot that helps diagnose people based on their symptoms and gives them instructions on what they should do next. We will have several phases in which we design the PMD chatbot and increase the complexity of its diagnosis over time.
