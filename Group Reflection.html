Group Reflection Together:

Overall, as a group, the last two assignments have been gratifying. Each of our members came from different backgrounds, like design, IT and information systems. Thanks to the diversity of our majors, we had a ton of options available to us when brainstorming ideas for our prototype and the many artefact options available. Communication was the key success factor in our group and what made the process of finishing the last 2 group assignments smoother. We stuck to Discord and Microsoft teams for our main point of communication, which helped build team synergy and ensure every member was up to date. We could have improved our communication further if face-to-face meetings were possible, as seeing each other in real life would make building team relationships better. Everyone was doing their tasks on time without having to send separate announcements each time a task needed to be completed. This made the progress of finishing our assignment on time possible. We believe one thing we could have improved in the group assignments was the unrealistic goals set in the agenda. Having unrealistic goals set can discourage members and make them rush trying to finish their parts. Leading to lower-quality work and pressure on the members.
