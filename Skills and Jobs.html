Skills & Jobs

To implement further improvements stated in the Scope and Limits section within six months with four team members, we must pick out individuals with unique skill sets to implement the changes effectively and efficiently.

Coder

As our current code for the medical chatbot utilises the python programming language, an experienced coder with a wide range of knowledge on python specifically is required for the further implementation of visual changes, a variety of platform availability and other coding-related changes.

AI Expert

Additionally, an AI expert is required to implement Artificial Intelligence in the chatbot to assist users with a realistic response further. The AI expert will have to include natural language processing (NLP) and other features relating to AI. Further improvements include a wide range of diseases, AI, UI updates, mobile applications, Approval from multiple countries, etc.

Research Specialist

To expand our range of diseases and symptoms to be added to the chatbot radar, a research specialist is required to keep the bot up-to-date.

Medical professional

To further verify the details provided by the Research specialist, a medical professional with a certified degree and experience is required to not only see if the details provided to the patients are valid but also allow the organisation/ team to follow legal obligations regarding medical pieces of advice.
